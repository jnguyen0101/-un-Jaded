Current Directors

Operations Department

Meibeth Cannon
Operations Director

Meibeth (she/her) is a third-year Government & Humanities student with a passion for human rights and social justice. Inspired by community renewal, personal freedom, and a hope for a more mindful world, she aims to foster greater respect around mental health care. As a Chinese American and a mental health advocate, she seeks to die into the intersectionality of the two to develop a greater understanding of her own heritage and to encourage vulnerability among the (un)Jaded community, especially concerning underrepresented struggles within mental health.

Areas of interest: exploring nature, music, cloud watching, crocheting

Kathy Nguyen
Events/Finance Director

Kathy (she/her) is a third-year Computational Biology major passionate about social advocacy and destigmatizing mental health in Asian communities. Inspired by the power of vulnerability and collective healing, she hopes to spark conversations that normalize mental health struggles and uplift underrepresented voices. She seeks to foster inclusive spaces within (un)Jaded that encourage open conversation, compassion, and support, while building stronger connections across communities.

Areas of interest: being outdoors, criminal psychology videos, cooking, listening to music

Program Department

Ruchi Sankolli
Program Director

Ruchi (she/her) is a fourth-year Health and Society student, with a minor in Health Communications. She is passionate about social equity and different cultures. She strives to increase access to mental health resources for underprivileged communities, while fostering a supportive and caring environment within the organization. Additionally, she aims to build a bridge that can connect culture and well-being, encouraging people to strengthen their sense of identity in the process.

Areas of interest: crocheting, writing, data science, history

Nashitha Azeez
Fundraising Director

Nashitha is a senior double majoring in Psychology and Human Development and Family Sciences. She is currently on the Pre-OT track. She joined (un)Jaded because she was passionate about the intersection of Asian-American identity and mental health. In her free time, she likes to read, watch shows/movies, creatively write, and paint (however, she's also down to try new things).

Savannah Pyle
Culture Director

Savannah (she/her) is a fourth-year Psychology and Business student with the ultimate goal of breaking the mental health stigma within AAPI households and communities. The intersectionality between identities plays an important role in her life — her mixed Vietnamese and white identities led her to help others discover their balance and find solace within their identities. She has found family within (un)Jaded, growing connections and friendships over the years she has been in the org, and hopes to grow this community.

Areas of interest: true crime/commentary videos, hiking, getting coffee, cute animal memes

Strategy Department

Asiyah Jilani
Marketing Director

Asiyah (she/her) is a fourth-year Informatics student. She is very passionate about open conversation and awareness of mental health issues, to combat stigma in her religious and cultural groups. She sees importance in connection, community, and discussion to develop solutions, and is excited to uphole those through (un)Jaded.

Annie Nguyen
Marketing Director

Annie (she/her) is a third-year Neuroscience major passionate about disability justice and healthcare accessibility. As the eldest daughter in a Vietnamese family and sister to a brother with special needs, she hopes to break down stigmas surrounding disability and mental health while creating spaces for open conversations and amplifying underrepresented voices within Asian communities and beyond. (un)Jaded has been a meaningful space for her growth, and she hopes to continue fostering open dialogue, challenging stigmas, and building an inclusive community within the org.

Areas of interest: digital art and design, art markets, rom-coms and dramas, swimming
