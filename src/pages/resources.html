Mental Health First Aid Training

Mental Health First Aid trains you to recognize and respond to signs of mental health and substance use challenges, providing initial support until professional help is available. The course covers risk factors, warning signs, crisis and non-crisis strategies, and resources for ongoing support.

988 Suicide & Crisis Hotline

The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline provides free and confidential emotional support to people in suicidal crisis or emotional distress 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, across the United States and its territories.

Call/Text 988

Chat at 988lifeline.org

Asian Americans Advancing Justice

AAJC is a national nonprofit founded in 1991 that works to advance the civil and human rights of Asian Americans and promote a fair, equitable society for all. Through education, litigation, and public policy advocacy, AAJC empowers the fastest-growing U.S. population to participate fully in democracy and ensures their voices are heard.

Asian Family Support Services of Austin (AFSSA)

Asian Family Support Services of Austin, or AFSSA (formerly SAHELI) is a nonprofit organization based in Austin, Texas, that provides assistance to Asian families dealing with domestic violence, sexual assault, and trafficking.
